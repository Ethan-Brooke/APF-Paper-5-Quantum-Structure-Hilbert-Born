# Do Not Claim — Paper 5

1. **Do not claim Paper 5 proves quantum mechanics from classical.** It derives the specific formalism (complex Hilbert + Born + CPTP) assuming admissibility; it does not reduce to classical physics.

2. **Do not claim Gleason's theorem is APF-specific.** It is a standard result; Paper 5 applies it.

3. **Do not claim Frobenius trichotomy rules out $\mathbb{H}$ in generality.** It rules out $\mathbb{H}$ for APF admissibility frameworks specifically, via $L_{\rm loc}$ commutativity.

4. **Do not claim decoherence is identical across interpretations.** Paper 5 frames decoherence as Type III regime exit; this is APF-specific content, not the full decoherence literature.

5. **Do not claim $L_{\rm spectral\_action\_internal}$ derives the full SM Lagrangian.** It derives the leading Seeley-DeWitt coefficients with structural values; complete Lagrangian includes terms beyond $a_0, a_2, a_4$.

6. **Do not claim $L_{\rm QG\_P1\_closure}$ is a theory of quantum gravity.** It is a UV completion as Type IV regime exit — structural, not a full framework.

7. **Do not treat Bohmian mechanics as an unrefuted alternative.** Paper 5 explicitly rules it out via countermodel analysis (Supp §countermodels).

8. **Do not claim the Sector A ↔ B measurement framing replaces standard measurement theory.** It is a reframing (tag $[P + framing]$) that makes the bridge-theorem structure visible.

9. **Do not claim Paper 5 derives spin-statistics theorem.** That is standard QFT; Paper 5 takes it.

10. **Do not claim $a_0 = 61$ is a new coincidence.** $61$ is Paper 2's $C_{\rm total}$; the spectral-action $a_0$ matching it is the structural content.
